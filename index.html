Welcome to Hesus Global Philippines

A spirit-filled church community made up of ordinary people from around the world whose lives are being transformed by the love, power, and grace of God.

People at Hesus Global are in all different stages of their journey with God – but we’re all journeying towards the same goal – to be transformed by God’s love, power, and grace.  It means living differently, loving differently, and putting our trust in doing things God’s way, instead of our own.  It’s normal for us to see people being transformed physically, emotionally, and spiritually as they discover how powerful God is and how much He loves them.
